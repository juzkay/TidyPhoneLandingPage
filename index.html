Frequently Asked Questions

What is TidyPhone?

TidyPhone is a simple, powerful iPhone storage cleaner designed to help you take control of your iPhone without risking the memories you want to keep. Clean your iPhone and reclaim storage space in seconds by deleting duplicate photos, compressing videos, removing screenshots, and cleaning contacts.

How does TidyPhone work?

TidyPhone scans your device and categorizes files by type, size, and quality. Everything is reviewed by you before deletion, and all processing happens directly on your device. Use the intuitive swipe interface to keep photos you love and delete duplicates, blurry shots, and clutter.

Is TidyPhone safe and private?

Yes, TidyPhone is 100% private and offline. All scans happen on your device, photos and contacts never leave your iPhone, there are no cloud uploads, no tracking or data collection. You have full control over what gets deleted, and everything is reviewed by you before permanent deletion.

Can I delete duplicate photos with TidyPhone?

Yes! TidyPhone automatically finds identical and similar photos, groups duplicates together for easy review, and helps you keep the best version while clearing burst photo clutter fast.

Does TidyPhone compress videos?

Yes, TidyPhone Pro includes video compression that can free up space without noticeable quality loss. You can preview before and after, choose exactly which videos to compress, and save gigabytes in minutes.

Will TidyPhone help with "Storage Almost Full" warnings?

Absolutely! TidyPhone is designed specifically to help you avoid "Storage Almost Full" warnings. With features like duplicate photo detection, screenshot cleanup, video compression, and large file finder, you can reclaim significant storage space quickly and easily.
